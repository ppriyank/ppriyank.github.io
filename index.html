Research

My research focuses on developing efficient training techniques for computer vision models, with a particular emphasis on enhancing the robustness against real-world noise (fog, rain, snow, etc. in autonomous driving). I primarily focus on Zero-Shot and VLMs / transformers , espcially Object Detectors. I have significant experience in interpretable and explanable AI as well.   On the side, I have beeen working (also, my Master's thesis) on Person Re-Identification (ReID) for past 7 years.
